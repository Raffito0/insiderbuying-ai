### 1. Apify Store — Niche Web Scraping Actors
**Ranking Score: 78/100**

#### What it does
Build and publish web scraping actors (automated scrapers) on the Apify Store marketplace. Each actor scrapes a specific website or data source (e.g., Google Maps businesses, job listings, real estate prices, social media profiles) and users pay per result or per event. Apify handles hosting, billing, discovery, and customer support.

#### Revenue model
- Who pays: Businesses needing structured web data — marketers, recruiters, real estate investors, researchers
- How much: $0.005-$0.05 per result (pay-per-event) or $29-199/month (rental model)
- Why they pay: Building and maintaining scrapers is painful — websites change layouts, add anti-bot measures. A maintained, working scraper is worth paying for
- Model: Usage-based (pay-per-event/result) or monthly rental

#### Customer acquisition
Apify Store has 50,000+ monthly active users who search for scrapers by website name or data type. Your actor appears in search results alongside competitors. Apify also features top actors in categories. Zero external marketing needed.

#### Revenue projection
- Month 1: EUR0-50 — Actors need time to get discovered; initial users testing free tiers
- Month 2: EUR50-200 — Early adopters; if scraper works reliably, word spreads in marketplace
- Month 3: EUR200-500 — Compound effect from multiple actors + returning customers
- Month 6: EUR500-1500 — With 3-5 published actors covering different niches
- **Pessimistic**: EUR100/month after 6 months (actors for saturated niches)
- **Optimistic**: EUR2000/month after 6 months (hit an underserved niche)
- Assumptions: 3-5 actors published, at least one serves an underserved niche, actors maintained when target sites change

#### Costs
- Setup: EUR0 (Creator Plan is $1/month for first 6 months, with $500 platform credits)
- Monthly: EUR5-20 (Apify Creator Plan + occasional proxy costs for testing)

#### Agent vs Human split
- Agent does: Build scrapers in Python, publish to Apify, monitor for breakages, auto-fix when site layouts change, respond to user issues via templates
- I do: Identify which niches/websites to target (initial research, ~2 hours). After build, review agent alerts weekly (~15 min)
- Build time: 1-2 weeks (3-5 actors)

#### Risks
1. **Target websites change their structure** — Mitigation: Agent monitors for failures and auto-adjusts selectors. Apify notifies when actors fail, giving time to fix
2. **Anti-bot protection blocks scrapers** — Mitigation: Apify provides built-in proxy rotation. Choose targets without Cloudflare (government sites, smaller platforms, non-tech businesses)
3. **Saturated niches (Google Maps, LinkedIn already have 50+ actors)** — Mitigation: Target non-English or niche platforms (Italian real estate portals, German job boards, niche B2B directories)

#### Evidence

#### Competitive landscape
- Free alternatives: Apify has free actors for popular sites (Google Maps, Facebook, etc.)
- Why someone pays us: Niche/non-English site scrapers have few or zero competitors. A well-maintained scraper for Italian real estate (Immobiliare.it), German jobs (StepStone.de), or niche B2B directories has near-zero competition

#### Churn & retention
