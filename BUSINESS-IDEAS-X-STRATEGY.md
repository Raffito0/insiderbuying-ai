### Can we detect viral tweets early?

**Technical feasibility: YES, with caveats.**

- Early engagement (first 30-60 min) is the strongest ranking signal
- 60-80% of lifetime impressions happen in first 2-6 hours
- Engagement velocity (likes/min in first 5 min) is a reliable predictor
- Multiple academic papers confirm: ViralBERT, engagement velocity models
- Python + X API can monitor specific accounts' new posts and check engagement rate

**But — can we AUTO-COMMENT early?**

⚠️ **NO. Automated replies are banned on X as of 2026.**

The viral surfing strategy as originally conceived (auto-detect → auto-reply) is NOT viable without HIGH ban risk. However:
- **Manual viral surfing IS viable**: the agent detects viral posts → sends alert to Telegram → human reviews and posts reply manually (30 sec effort)
- **Automated CONTENT that references trending topics IS viable**: the agent posts its own tweet about the same topic (not a reply)

**API costs for monitoring 50 accounts:**
- 50 accounts × 24 checks/day = 1,200 user lookups/day × $0.01 = $12/day
- Plus reading their tweets: ~200 reads/day × $0.005 = $1/day
- Total: ~$13/day = ~$390/month ← TOO EXPENSIVE for monitoring

**Cheaper alternative**: Monitor RSS feeds / webhook integrations / Nitter mirrors — $0/month. Or monitor only top 10-15 accounts = ~$80/month.

## ⚠️ CRITICAL STRATEGY REVISION

**The original prompt assumed automated replies on X. Research shows this is a HIGH BAN RISK in 2026.**

X's Feb 2026 policy: "If a human is not tapping on the screen, the account and all associated accounts will likely be suspended."

**What the agent CAN do safely (100% automated):**
1. ✅ Post original tweets (scheduled via OAuth — Typefully, Buffer, Hypefury)
2. ✅ Post threads, images, polls
3. ✅ Create content with AI (GPT-4.1 mini generates, agent schedules)
4. ✅ Monitor trends and create reactive content (post about trending topic, NOT reply to it)
5. ✅ Full Telegram automation (Bot API, no restrictions)
6. ✅ Telegram group engagement (bot answers questions, posts content)

**What the agent CANNOT do safely:**
1. ❌ Automated replies to other users' tweets
2. ❌ Automated likes
3. ❌ Automated follows
4. ❌ Auto-commenting on viral posts (reply guy strategy)

**Revised strategy: Content-First Brand Building**
Instead of reply marketing, the agent builds the brand through:
- High-quality original content (3-5 posts/day)
- Data-driven/provocative posts that attract engagement organically
- Reactive content (post about what's trending, as original content)
- Telegram as the engagement + conversion channel
- Bio link as the monetization funnel

This is slower for growth (3-6 months to 1K followers vs potentially faster with replies) but SUSTAINABLE and ban-proof.
